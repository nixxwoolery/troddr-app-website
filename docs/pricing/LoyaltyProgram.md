# TRODDR Insider Partner Pricing

## Founding Partner Offer

Available for a limited number of early partners.

Founding Partners receive locked-in pricing for 12 months and early access to future TRODDR loyalty and marketing features.

# What's Included

Every TRODDR Insider Partner receives:

- TRODDR Insider offer listing
- Loyalty rewards program setup
- NFC-enabled loyalty card support
- QR code redemption support
- Partner dashboard access
- Customer redemption tracking
- Offer management tools
- Featured discovery opportunities
- Monthly performance reporting
- Staff onboarding and training

---

# Example Insider Rewards

Businesses choose their own reward structure.

Examples include:

- Free drink
- Complimentary appetizer
- Free side dish
- Free dessert
- Birthday reward
- Members-only special
- Buy 5 visits, receive a reward
- VIP access or priority benefits

# Founding Partner Benefits

- Locked-in pricing for 12 months
- Founding Partner badge within TRODDR
- Early access to new platform features
- Priority consideration for promotional campaigns
- Opportunity to help shape future loyalty features

# Enterprise

Businesses with six or more locations, franchise groups, hotel groups, or destination portfolios can request a custom enterprise package.
