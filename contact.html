Frequently Asked Questions

When will the TRODDR app be available?

We're currently in development and plan to launch before the end of 2025. Join our waitlist to be notified when we're ready!

How can I list my business on TRODDR?

We're selectively curating authentic experiences. Contact us at partnerships@troddr.com with details about your business.

Do you cover areas outside Jamaica?

We're starting with Jamaica and plan to expand across the Caribbean. Follow us for updates on new destinations.

How do you choose recommendations?

Our team of local experts and travelers personally vet every recommendation to ensure authentic, high-quality experiences.

Can I contribute content to TRODDR?

Absolutely! We love working with local experts and passionate travelers. Reach out to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Is TRODDR free to use?

The core TRODDR experience will be free. We may offer premium features in the future, but our mission is to make authentic travel accessible to everyone.
