Frequently asked

Questions before you join?

Is TRODDR for Business only for Rewards partners?

No. TRODDR for Business is the home for all business tools, including listing management, bookings, analytics, feedback, promotions and Rewards. Your dashboard displays the capabilities enabled for your account.

Can I manage more than one location?

Yes. Eligible multi-location businesses can view individual locations and combined company-level information from one account.

How do I get dashboard access?

Approved businesses receive a private invitation. You’ll use it to create your account, review any applicable agreement and complete setup.

Do all businesses receive the same tools?

No. Access depends on your business type, agreement and selected products. This keeps the dashboard focused on what your team actually uses.
