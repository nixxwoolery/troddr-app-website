Caribbean Travel, Reimagined

TRODDR was born from a simple belief: the best travel experiences come from local knowledge, shared with genuine passion. We're building a platform that connects you with the Caribbean the way locals experience it.

Our Story

"TRODDR started long before it had a name. Back when I was the go-to friend to call for anyone visiting Negril."

If you asked where to go for a quick lunch, I'd send you straight to Corner Bar for stewed conch and fried chicken. For breakfast? Kuyaba, and don't even think about skipping the freshly squeezed orange juice. If you went back for dinner it was the Lobster Casino and creamy seafood pasta, no question. And of course, I'd make sure you knew about the escovitch fish at Pushcart, big enough to feed two (but you'll want it all to yourself).

It was never about ticking off tourist checklists. It has always been about those experiences that make a trip unforgettable. The kind of spots you only find out about from someone who's already been there, loved it, and can't wait to share it. Places you'll still be talking about long after you're home.

That's why I created TRODDR, to make sure you get those recommendations too. Like you're exploring with a friend showing you around, not following a guidebook.
